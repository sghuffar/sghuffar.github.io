About Me

I am currently a Tenured Associate Professor at the Department of Space Science, Institute of Space Technology, Pakistan. My research focuses on the intersection of photogrammetry, remote sensing, and artificial intelligence for Earth observation applications. I completed my PhD at Vienna University of Technology, Austria under Prof. Dr. Norbert Pfeifer, and was a research fellow at the University of St Andrews, UK, working with Prof. Dr. Tobias Bolch on automated processing of historical satellite stereo imagery. I received the Erasmus Mundus Scholarship for my MSc in Space Science and Technology from Germany and Sweden.
